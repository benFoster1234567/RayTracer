--We need a scene with a camera, a light and a sphere. Sphere is scaled to 2 and is red with a material.
--We also need a material for the sphere and a plane. The plane should be scaled and the plane should 
have the checkered pattern

Example:

Camera:

World:
    Objects: 
        - Sphere:
            Transform:
                Scale: {x: 2, y: 2, z: 2} 
            Material: 
                Ambient: 0.1 
                Diffuse: 0.9 
                Specular: 0.9 
                Shininess: 200 
                Color: {r: 1, g: 0, b: 0} 
    AreaLight: 
        corner: {x: y: z:} 
        uVector: {x: y: z:} 
        uSteps: 10 
        vVector: {x: y: z:} 
        vSteps: 10 
        intensity: {r: g: b:}
